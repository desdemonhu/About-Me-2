About Me

I was born and raised in on the south side of Chicago and still consider that to be my true home. After college I spent two years living in the mountains of Jackson Hole, Wyoming and became a true lover of all things outdoors. After returning to live in Chicago for several years, I met my husband, Bryce, and moved to Seattle.

I work in a tech startup and working with engineers got me interested in learning to code. I started on my own using Free Code Camp and other online sources. Wanting to progress more quickly, I recently signed up for classes at CodeFellows here in Seattle.
